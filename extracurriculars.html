F1Tenth

There are several fascinating teams under RoboNotts, from healthcare assistant robots to search-and-rescue dogs. The team I have selected is F1Tenth!

"F1TENTH is an international community of researchers, engineers, and autonomous systems enthusiasts." The name comes from building a 1/10th scale F1 car, and designing it as an autonomous vehicle system. And this isn't just for show! There are several competitions held every year across the globe, spanning from Japan to the US and between. All with the amazing opportunity to race against other team's cars.


I cannot wait to delve into the world of autonomous systems with my F1Tenth team. (Click here for F1Tenth website!)

Powerlifting

My dedication in powerlifting started around two years ago. What started as an activity that I forced myself to do to "be more productive", has now become an important part of my life. It has taught me discipline, perseverance, and to love difficulty and struggle. What I love most about powerlifting is how rewarding it can feel, especially when seeing your hard work pay off. Truly the epitome of "no pain, no gain".
